PORTAL



OCTOBER 10, 2007...


Valve invites you to experience the

PORTAL

20th Anniversary Update


exciting, never-before-seen insight into the making of Portal!

Our Half-Life 25th anniversary documentary and subsequent Half-Life 2 20th anniversary documentary went so well that we decided to make yet another entry in the series.
It's about how what started as a small team of university students with a short demo, managed to impress the Valve CEO Gabe Newell so much that they were hired on the spot to make a full game at Valve!

STEAM WORKSHOP SUPPORT

The Portal 20th Anniversary Update introduces Steam Workshop support, allowing players to create and share their own custom maps and mods.
The possibilities are endless, and the community has already created hundreds of new levels and challenges for you to enjoy.
Download them now and experience Portal like never before!

UPDATED GAME CONTENT

The Portal 20th Anniversary Update features 15 new challenge levels, new story elements, voice lines, Easter eggs, and hidden secrets.
We've also restored beta content and added new achievements and challenges for you to unlock.



Updated Graphics settings

The Portal 20th Anniversary Update features enhanced graphics with updated textures and models, improved lighting and shadow effects, and support for higher resolutions and widescreen displays.
Experience Portal like never before with stunning visuals and improved performance.

Gamepad, Input, and Steam Deck Updates

The Portal 20th Anniversary Update adds support for modern controllers, improved mouse and keyboard controls, and customizable key bindings.
The game is now Steam Deck Verified and supports Steam Input for a seamless experience on the go.
